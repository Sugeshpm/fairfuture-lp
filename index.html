Don’t Let Myths Decide Your Future.

Myth: Canada no longer welcomes international students.

Reality: Canada continues to offer world-class education with strong post-study stay options for skilled students.

Myth: It’s impossible to get PR after studies.

Reality: Canada still provides clear pathways to PR for graduates in high-demand fields.

Myth: Immigration rules are always complicated and uncertain.

Reality: With proper guidance and planning, students can turn the immigration process into a clear, achievable roadmap.

Myth: Only big cities like Toronto or Vancouver are worth studying in.

Reality: Many smaller cities in Canada offer affordable living, high-quality institutions, and excellent career opportunities

Myth: You can’t work while studying in Canada.

Reality: Most programs allow part-time work during studies and full-time work during breaks

Myth: Tuition in Canada is unaffordable for most international students.

Reality: With scholarships, co-op programs, and careful planning, students can access world-class education without breaking the bank.
